About Prosperity360

A global development foundation headquartered in Africa and working across the Global South to uplift communities through innovation, economic empowerment, resilient livelihoods, climate solutions, health systems, and education transformation.

Who We Are

Our Identity

Prosperity360 Foundation is a Pan-African, Global South–rooted development institution that accelerates multi-dimensional prosperity, systems transformation, and community-driven development across Africa, Latin America, South Asia, and other emerging regions.

Our Belief

Prosperity is not a single metric; it is a constellation:

  • Income
  • Inclusion
  • Resilience
  • Opportunity
  • Dignity
  • Voice

Our Philosophy

"Prosperity grows when people, systems, and institutions work in harmony."

We do not just "run projects or programs." We build systems, strengthen institutions, and empower communities.

Founder's Speech

There comes a time in every generation when ordinary people must rise to confront extraordinary inequality. When the world must pause and ask: how can we live in a land of abundance, and yet, so many remain poor, unemployed, or unseen? I was born in that paradox.

Prosperity360 is not just another NGO. It is a movement; a new model for inclusive prosperity through innovation, empowerment, and community-driven change. I believe that when people are equipped to create value, communities can design their own future.

We reject the myth of scarcity. Africa is not poor, it is underconnected. It is not lacking wealth, it is lacking alignment. Prosperity360 exists to align people, purpose, and possibility.

Ridwan Awosanya
Founder, Prosperity360 Foundation
